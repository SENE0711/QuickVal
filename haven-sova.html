Haven - Sova Attack

A Long Early Recon

This dart reduces the risk of being picked by enemy team when crossing A lobby, adding pressure to enemy A long players.

A Site Recon

This dart clears difficult angles in A short and pressuring the backsite enemy so the team could flood out from A long and short safely.

B Entrance Recon

This dart clears the most dangerous angles that is stopping players from going B site, can be used early in the round for taking mid space.

C Site Recon

This dart clears the backsite and the close corners of C site, it lands at a spot that is difficult to break, which put a big pressure on the enemy team.

Haven - Sova Defend

A Lobby Early Recon

This dart lands above the enemy A lobby player, it is super useful for early round infomation. It is difficult to break.

A Site Retake

This dart clears site, graffiti and a small portion of hell. It is difficult to break and enemy players usually don't have time to break it under pressure.

C Site Retake

This dart lands at the same place as where the attack dart lands. So when your team is retaking, this dart will be unexpected by the enemy team.
